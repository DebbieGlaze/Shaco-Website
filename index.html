Shaco

The Demon Jester

"How about a magic trick? The jokes on you.."

Shaco, the Demon Jester, is an infamous assassin who thrives on chaos and confusion. His abilities allow him to deceive and outmaneuver opponents while dealing massive burst damage.

Abilities

Deceive (Q) Cooldown: 8 seconds

Shaco becomes invisible for a brief duration and teleports to the target nearby location.

Jack In The Box (W) Cooldown: 16 seconds

Shaco creates a hidden trap that fears nearby enemies when triggered, attacking them for magic damage.

Two-Shiv Poison (E) Cooldown: 8 seconds

Shaco throws a dagger dealing damage slowing the target.

Hallucinate (R) Cooldown: 100 seconds

Shaco becomes invisible briefly while creating an uncontrollable clone of himself that deals reduced damage.
